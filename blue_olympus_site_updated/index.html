About the Game

theworldofolympus is a virtual labor economy built from the ground up by a founder who started with nothing. Players build worlds, mine crypto, complete missions, and reshape how work and value are defined — all within an immersive metaverse powered by AI and blockchain.

Gameplay Features

  • 💠 Build your own planet or virtual world through in-game schools and tools
  • 💰 Mine cryptocurrencies tied to real-world coins (BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.)
  • 🎯 Complete corporate-sponsored missions that affect real-world outcomes
  • 🏗️ Hire, teach, or partner with other players to grow economies
  • 📈 Submit investment ideas and watch AI simulate their impact
